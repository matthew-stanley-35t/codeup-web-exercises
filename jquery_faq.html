How many acres of land are considered protected in America as National Park land?
The National Park Service protects over 84 million acres of wild landscapes and historic sites.
Which National Park was created first and is the oldest?
Yellowstone National Park was founded in 1872 and is the oldest National Park.
Which National park has the best Waterfalls?
Three of the ten highest waterfalls in the world are inside of Yosemite National Park in California.
Which National Park is the largest of the National Parks?
Wrangell-St. Elias National Park, Alaska, is the largest, covering 13,000 square miles or 13.2 million acres.
Which is the smallest national park?
The smallest national park is Hot Springs National Park in Arkansas, coming in at about 5,500 acres.
Is it possible to visit 2 parks in one day?
You can visit two parks in a day if you visit Yellowstone National Park and Grand Teton National Park as they are only 10 miles apart in Wyoming.
Which National Park has the most visitors annually?
Grand Canyon National Park saw about 4.53 million visitors last year.
Which State has the most National Parks?
California has the most with 9 national parks.
Which National Parks were used as sets for Star Wars?
Death Valley National Park and Redwood National Park were both used as sets while filming the original Star Wars Trilogy.
What is the newest park?
Pinnacles National Park in California was designated on January 10, 2013 and is America's newest national park.
